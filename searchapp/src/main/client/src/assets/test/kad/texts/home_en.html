Knihy a dějiny

The journal Knihy a dějiny (Books and history) is a scientific peer-reviewed periodical issued by the Library of the Czech Academy of Sciences since 1994. It is comes out once a year, focusing on the publication of the results of research into the history of books, book printing and libraries in Bohemia and Moravia until the mid-19th century. Besides source-based and material studies, the journal publishes reviews and reports on domestic as well as foreign bibliographic production and provides information on subject-related conferences and exhibitions. It is published in Czech, but peer-reviewed articles are accompanied by an English translation of the abstract, keywords and summary.
