Citation guidelines

 

For the sake of smooth progress of editorial work, the editorial board asks you to adhere to these guidelines when writing your manuscripts:

  • the maximum length should not exceed 20 standard pages (i.e. 1,800 characters per page including spaces and footnotes) in the section of Studies; 2.5 standard pages (4,500 characters including spaces) for a review and 1.5 standard pages (2,700 characters including spaces) for a report;

  • the submitted article must be complemented by a summary not exceeding one page, a short abstract (a of 1,016 characters) and keywords (translation will be provided by the editorial board);

  • the authors who plan to accompany their articles with pictures must send the editorial board a statement on the ownership of copyright for their printed as well as electronic publication; Pictorial supplements must be submitted in print quality (with a resolution of at least 300 dpi);

  • send your articles written in Word 97, Word 2000 or other text editors to vesela@knav.cz or  knihyadejiny@knav.cz.
